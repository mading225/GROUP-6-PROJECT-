#  Movie Studio Market Analysis
### Exploratory Data Analysis for Strategic Film Production Decisions

##  Project Overview
Our company is launching a new movie studio and needs data-driven guidance on what types of films to produce.  
This project uses historical movie data to identify the characteristics of films that perform best financially and with audiences.

By analyzing box office revenue, ratings, popularity, genres, runtimes, and studio performance, we generate actionable business recommendations to reduce risk and maximize profitability.

---

##  Business Goal
Help the new studio answer:

- What genres generate the most revenue?
- What movie characteristics attract audiences?
- What production strategies improve box office success?

The final outcome is a set of **evidence-based recommendations** to guide movie production and investment decisions.

## Key Insights
- Action,Adventure and Sci-fi films consistently generate the highest revenue
- Higher audience ratings strongly correlate with higher box office gross
- Movies of 120 minutes and higher perform best financially
- Popularity metrics help predict financial success
- Major studios dominate market share

---

## Recommendations
- Prioritize high-grossing genres (Action, Adventure, Sci-fi)
- Invest in production quality to improve ratings
- Optimize runtimes of 120 minutes and higher.
- Use audience popularity metrics for greenlighting projects
- Benchmark strategies from top studios
